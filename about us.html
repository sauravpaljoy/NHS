Vision And Mission:

To aid students in developing mature sense of obligation to the creator, country and their fellowmen, to impart sound education, to form praiseworthy character,
and to develop in the students the habits of true discipline and self efficiency to enable them to become dutiful and patriotic citizens of Bangladesh.


Canteen:

Without good food we cannot expect good health and a sound nation ultimately. Considering health issues of the students, we have set canteen in each academic building.
Healthy and quality foods with lots of variety are available in these canteens. Therefore, students need not go outside and consume roadside unhygienic foods.


Auditorium:

We have a huge auditorium fully furnished with modern amenities. Sitting at the auditorium students can hear and watch various cultural programmes,
debate competition and many more scheduled and unscheduled seminars, discussions programme .


Library

A school library is a vital portion of a school. Without a school library a school remains incomplete. It is one of the most important things in an educational institution.
Our school has also an enlarged library. It consists two parts. One part is used as an official room and another part is use as an reading room as well as a book collection room.
Our school library remains open from 10 am to 4 pm.
