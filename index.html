Nimsar High School(NHS)

Nimsar High School(NHS) is an academic institute located at Nimsar,Burichang upozila Cumilla.It is a combind School.Its a most famous school in cumilla Distrct.

Its institute code (EIIN) is 105270.It was established on 01 january,1958.

Its co-education type is Combind.The institute has following 3 disciplines:Science,Business Studies and Humanities.

Average exam passing rate of the institute is:JSC/Equivalent:89.1%,SSc/Equivalent:92.52%.Its MPO number is 808051301.It's MPO level is Secondary. It has day shift.


Curriculum

There are Bengali-medium courses of study in three programmes: humanities, science, and commerce. As of 2014, the school had 50 teachers. Enrollment is approximately 1000.

Both boys and girls are admitted.The college section admits students from all branches of the school and from other areas of Bangladesh.
